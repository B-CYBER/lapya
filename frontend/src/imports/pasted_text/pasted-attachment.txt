═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
DESIGN INTENT — read twice
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

This is NOT a typical SaaS landing page. We are building something that
looks like the digital cover of a premium wellness magazine that happens
to also be a product.

The page must FEEL like:
- A New York Times Magazine feature about food and health
- Crossed with the editorial polish of Headspace or Calm
- With the cultural warmth of an African Vogue cover
- Anchored by the clinical credibility of Mayo Clinic

It must NOT feel like:
- A vibecoded centered-hero one-button page
- A Bootstrap template with a gradient blob
- A generic AI-design with three identical icon cards
- A fitness app with neon colors

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
LAYOUT PHILOSOPHY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

- ASYMMETRY over symmetry. Break the grid in 3-4 places intentionally.
- EDITORIAL RHYTHM: alternate dark Navy sections with warm white sections
  with full-bleed photo sections — no two adjacent sections look the same.
- MAGAZINE COLUMN WIDTHS: vary between 12-col, 8-col, and 6-col layouts
  per section. Never lock to a single grid.
- WHITESPACE is the luxury. Use 120-160px section padding on desktop.
- TYPOGRAPHY HIERARCHY: at least 3 size jumps per section (display →
  pull-quote → body).
- A subtle "issue / chapter" feel — small uppercase eyebrow labels above
  each major section, like a magazine ("CHAPTER 01 — THE PROBLEM").
- Tiny editorial flourishes: numbered captions under images
  ("01 / Mama Nkechi, 52, Enugu"), small drop caps on opening paragraphs,
  thin Marigold horizontal rules between sections.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Desktop (1440px wide) — primary canvas
2. Tablet (768px) — adapt the asymmetry intentionally, don't just stack
